About HLANZEKA

ESTABLISHED 2023

Innovative Waste Management Solutions

HLANZEKA Waste Management is a newly launched company in 2023, bringing fresh ideas and modern technology to the waste management industry. Our "Trash to Cash" philosophy is at the core of everything we do, turning environmental responsibility into economic opportunity.

Based in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, we're committed to transforming waste management practices with efficient, sustainable solutions that create value from waste materials.

As a new company, we're agile, responsive, and committed to providing personalized service to each of our clients. We're building our reputation on transparency, efficiency, and delivering real financial returns from waste management.

Our Services

Recycling Solutions

Comprehensive recycling programs for paper, plastic, glass, metal, and electronics. We maximize recovery rates to increase your revenue from recyclables.

Industrial Waste Management

Customized waste handling solutions for manufacturing and industrial facilities, including hazardous material management and byproduct recovery.

Waste Collection & Transport

Reliable scheduled and on-demand waste collection services with modern, eco-friendly vehicles equipped for all types of waste materials.

Waste-to-Energy Conversion

Advanced technology to convert non-recyclable waste into clean energy, reducing landfill use while generating power and revenue.

Waste Audit & Consulting

Detailed waste stream analysis to identify cost-saving opportunities and improve sustainability performance for your business.

Sustainability Programs

Development and implementation of comprehensive sustainability initiatives to reduce environmental impact and enhance corporate responsibility.
